What is MinerSpark?

MinerSpark is a research cohort designed to give freshmen and sophomores (and motivated juniors) an entry point into engineering research. The cohort is made possible through a collaboration with CREATE (Center for Research in Engineering and Technology).

Students form interdisciplinary teams across engineering majors (up to 3 teams of 4 members) and work toward producing a research paper and a poster presentation.
