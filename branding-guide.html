UI Components & Shapes

Event

Modern Squircle Card

Use a border radius of 20px for large cards, and 16px for smaller inner components. Use #F1F6FA as the background and apply a soft shadow.

Design Rules

  • Borders: When placing cards on white or gray backgrounds, use a subtle 1px border (#DCE7F0) to give it a crisp edge.
  • Shadows: Use soft, colored shadows rather than harsh black. For example, a shadow for a navy card should have a hint of navy in the RGB value.
  • Spacing: Use generous whitespace padding (usually 16px to 24px inside cards).
  • Highlights: Reserve the SHPE Orange specifically for interactive elements (buttons, active tabs, unread badges) to draw the user's eye.
