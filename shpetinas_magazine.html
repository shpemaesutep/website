What is SHPEtinas?

SHPEtinas is a supportive community within SHPE//MAES dedicated to empowering women in STEM through mentorship, professional development, and meaningful connections.

We create spaces where women feel seen, supported, and confident — through workshops, socials, outreach, and honest conversations.

Community is built in the small moments: checking in after exams, sharing internship leads, celebrating wins, and learning together. SHPEtinas exists to make sure you never have to do STEM alone.
