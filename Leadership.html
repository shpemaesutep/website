SHPE//MAES Board

WHO ARE WE?

We are a dedicated team of leaders from the UTEP SHPE/MAES chapter. Our mission is to foster an inclusive community where aspiring engineers and scientists can thrive. Through collaboration, guidance, and innovation, we aim to inspire positive change and empower our members to excel in their pursuits. Join us as we work together to shape a brighter future for all.
