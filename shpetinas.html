WHAT IS SHPEtinas?

The Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE) and Latinos in Science and Engineering (MAES) are two non-profit organizations committed to the advancement of Hispanics in STEM fields. SHPE and MAES were both founded in Los Angeles, California, in 1974. Over time, SHPE has grown to have over 11,000 members affiliated with 44 professional chapters and 245 student chapters across the nation. Similarly, MAES has expanded to include 40 student chapters nationwide, with their numbers increasing every year. In 1977, the UTEP student chapter of SHPE was chartered in conjunction with MAES by the then-president Richard Martinez within the University's College of Engineering.

MISSION

Our mission is to develop socially responsible STEM professionals who excel in their fields and positively impact our country. We strive to inspire and support individuals in becoming role models within our community, leading with expertise and dedication to create a lasting positive influence.

VISION

Our vision is to be the preeminent organization that shapes socially responsible STEM professionals, empowering them to make significant contributions to our nation, and inspiring them to serve as influential role models within our community.
